Methodology

How EquiLens generates analyses and what limits apply.

Pipeline

1. Extracts text from news URLs, YouTube videos, or X/Twitter posts.

2. Runs a structured model prompt to generate summary, bias scores, rationale, and global perspective.

3. Applies source calibration and stores metadata for transparency and history.

Limitations

Outputs are model-generated and may be wrong or incomplete.

Global perspective is a synthesis, not direct polling of world opinion.

Some websites block extraction or provide insufficient text.
